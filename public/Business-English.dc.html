Anchoring the first offer
In a negotiation, the first concrete number or scope you name becomes the anchor — the reference everything else is judged against. A vague opener gives the anchor away.
Weak vs. anchored
"We'll try to get it done soon."
"I can commit the core build by Friday, and the rest by Tuesday."
